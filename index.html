Anime And Its Influence

By: Conor Wright

Anime is growing in presence here in America and it has a strong influence on our youth. However, this is not necessarily a bad thing. Anime helps kids develop new interests and choose their career paths. It can even help form their moral stance in the world. Some artists learn from it and develop ways to express themselves through anime and manga. Unfortunately, the effects it has on your life aren't always positive. People can fail to develop proper social skills or even attempt the negative actions they witness in anime.
